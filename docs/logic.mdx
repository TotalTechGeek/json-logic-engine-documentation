Operators | Instruction 
-- | -- 
Greater Than | >
Greater Than Equals  | >=
Less Than | &lt;
Less Than Equals | &lt;=
Not | not, !
!! (to Boolean) | !!
And | and
Or | or
?? | ??
Equals | ==
Equals Strict | ===
Not Equals | !=
Not Equals Strict | !==
If | if, ?:
